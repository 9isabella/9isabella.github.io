Isabella Lombino

Isabella Lombino was born in MA some years ago. She grew up in a small town where she spent her time playing with friends, Nordic skiing, chasing frogs, and making delicious food with her family. She attended Northfield Mount Hermon for high school where she played soccer, tennis, Ultimate Frisbee, and Nordic skied. She also was a techie for numerous shows in the theatre. In her junior year, she co-founded a social enterprise, Shampoo Shuttle, which helps to alliviate the stigma and hygenic challenges of homeless people. She now attends Tufts University, studying computer science and entrepreneurship. She also plays for EWO, the Tufts Ultimate Frisbee team.
